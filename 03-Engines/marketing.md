# Marketing Engine

## Purpose
Amplify any goal through strategic content and outreach.

## Trigger
`/run marketing [goal-name]`

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Understand
1. What's the goal? (job search, product launch, newsletter growth, etc.)
2. Who's the audience?
3. What channels make sense?
4. What assets exist?

### Phase 2: Strategy
Generate marketing plan:
- Key messages (3 max)
- Content pillars
- Channel strategy
- Cadence recommendation

### Phase 3: Content Calendar
Create `04-Outputs/marketing/[goal]-calendar.md`:
- Weekly content themes
- Specific post ideas
- Repurposing opportunities
- Engagement tactics

### Phase 4: Execute
For each content piece:
- Draft with [[Knowledge]] context
- Optimize for channel
- Schedule/publish tracking
- Engagement follow-up

## Channel Playbooks

### LinkedIn (Job Search)
- Profile optimization checklist
- Post templates (story, insight, question)
- Comment strategy
- Connection outreach templates

### Twitter/X (Thought Leadership)
- Thread structures
- Engagement windows
- Quote tweet opportunities
- List building

### Newsletter (Audience Building)
- Subject line formulas
- Content structure
- CTA placement
- Growth tactics

### Blog (SEO/Authority)
- Keyword targeting
- Content clusters
- Internal linking
- Distribution checklist

## Metrics to Track
- Reach/impressions
- Engagement rate
- Click-throughs
- Conversions (goal-specific)
- Follower growth

## Integration with Summaries
Weekly summary includes:
- Top performing content
- Engagement highlights
- Suggested pivots
- Next week focus
